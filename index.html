About me

Currently I am a Senior Manager in American Express Company AI Labs, working on the company's Natural Langaue Processing R&D. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from University of Michigan, Electrical Engineering and Computer Science department, majoring in Control Systems, under the guidance of Professor Semyon M. Meerkov, in August 2021, and the B.S.E degree from Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT), China, in 2016.

Research

My research interests include:

  • Natural Language Processing,

  • Stochastic System Analysis and Control,

  • Application of Probability Theory,

  • Optimization.
